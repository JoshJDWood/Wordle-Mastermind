Wordle Mastermind

Welcome to Wordle Mastermind

In this game you must guess the 5 letter word. For each guess you give you will receive a green box for a correct letter in the correct space and a yellow square for a correct letter in the incorrect space. Use these hints to help you decipher the target word.

Right click on the letters in the guesses and keyboard to change their colour. I suggest colour coding:

  • GREY for Incorrect letters
  • YELLOW for Correct letter
  • GREEN for Correct letters in the correct position
